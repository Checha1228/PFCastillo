Laura & Simón

Julio 15, 2023

You know when they say “my dog is going to be with us getting ready and just hanging out for part of the day,” that its going to be a GOOD day! I’m a golden retriever lover, so having Courtney and Tim’s dog Millie there for most of the day, was a real treat!
I met Courtney and Tim for their engagement session last fall, and we had so much fun together I just knew their wedding day would be nothing short of amazing! They are both laid-back individuals, but care about the plan that’s in place (my type of people!!), they love their friends and family so much, and the love they share for one another is absolutely contagious. They are a joy to be around and their wedding day really mirrored who they are as a couple!

María & Pablo

November 12, 2022

This wedding day was nothing short of incredible!! From getting ready moments with the girls, to golden hour with the happy couple, to the neon sign with their last name on it, to a live band at the dance, it was PERFECTION. My little photographer heart was just bursting!
Ali & Andrew are one special couple. They are genuine, relaxed, and ready to celebrate. They truly enjoyed every single aspect of the day, and it is so evident in these photos! One of my favorite moments of the day was getting ready with the girls. They used the gorgeous space at Lady Vamp Studio, who also did their makeup, and Ali did a special “first look” with her girls! Ali hadn’t seen herself yet, so went to the mirror and just cried tears of joy! Overwhelmed a bit by everything coming together and realizing “this is happening!” There’s something so special about the moment you put your dress on and it all feels real. SO sweet.
